My journey

I started making arts when I was a kid and I found myself enjoying, eventually it became a hobby then turned into passion.

I remember that I used mouse to draw my first digital illustration and now I'm using a graphic tablet, it is very convenient to me as an artist, I tried and made a progress. Looking at my past arts now makes me think that "did my art style really improved?" I do doubt myself sometimes but seeing my outputs and how my viewers see my art it really makes my heart flutter and make more arts. I've been lost for a long time and I see myself losing my passion, but now I realize that I should try again, so that I could find the gift that had been lost.
